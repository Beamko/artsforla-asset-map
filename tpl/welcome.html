Welcome to CAM

Cultural Asset Map

CAM is an organic mapping project that identifies cultural resources along LA's Metro lines, municipal and school district boundaries. We built this tool to identify, connect and grow cultural resources throughout each of the 88 cities and 81 school districts that comprise Los Angeles County. The prototype launched in October 2013 with 1,000 sites recorded. It is our goal to expand this number exponentially through intentional community outreach and crowd sourcing. Please take a moment to test the prototype. It is a functional database that will allow users to sort criteria using an array of filters including organization size, type discipline as well as, geographic, metro line and municipal boundaries.

The depth and breadth of cultural activity exists on many layers and success of this project relies on contributed content. We hope to capture multiple layers of our regions expansive cultural ecology especially traditional, informal, community-based, spiritual, geographically as well as culturally diverse centers of creative expression.

Once you've taken it for a spin, please share your thoughts in the comment section. We specifically would like to know:

  • How you can envision using the tool?
  • What other types of information would be useful?
  • Who is missing?

If you would like to contribute a data-set have any questions or would like additional information, please contact Arts for LA.
